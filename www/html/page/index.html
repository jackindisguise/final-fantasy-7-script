About:

I created this project for the purposes of studying Japanese.
I wanted to be able to read something I enjoyed in Japanese, and Final Fantasy VII is probably my favorite piece of Japanese media.

Note: All English that appears in the script is taken directly from the official English translation.
If there are any mistaken or missing translations, that's because that's how it appears (or doesn't appear) in the official English translation of Final Fantasy VII.

Don't blame me.
